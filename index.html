The CHIKINNI Story

Born in the heart of Akure, CHIKINNI was built on one simple belief, that great food should be accessible to everyone. What started as a single restaurant has grown into one of Nigeria's most loved dining brands.

Every meal we serve carries the same passion, the same quality and the same love that built this brand from the ground up. We are not just a restaurant, we are an experience.
